The rules of the game are simple. Just follow these rules:

1. Wait for the order on the green board
2. Try to make required burger as quick as possible by using meat and oven on the right and ingredients on the left
3. Once your burger (hopefully with appropriate ingredients) is finished, ring the bell in order to start with new burger order
4. Repeat this until your shift is over
5. Wait for your paycheck!

Hurry, because you have only two minutes to finish all orders!
